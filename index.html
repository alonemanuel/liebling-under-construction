The Accelerator "Under Construction" is a project of Liebling Haus LAB, which is carried out in cooperation with Desertech Climate Innovation Center, Reality Proptech and the Bundesstiftung Bauakademie (Federal Bauakademie Foundation).

02 | Mission

We unite German and Israeli Con-, Prop-, and ClimateTech startups to co-create real-world-ready pilot plans that reduce negative impacts on people, planet, and profit, inspiring a shift towards more sustainable building practices for current and future crises in the Western Negev desert and in the world.

03 | Approach

Join a bilateral program to shape the future of construction

We identified three levers to transform the building sector and to build (back) better. If you are active in one of them - our program offers a once in a lifetime opportunity.
01 New Building Materials
A commitment to sustainability and resilience, our focus on new building materials involves pioneering the use of eco-friendly and recycled materials that reduce environmental impact and enhance the durability of constructions in challenging climates.
02
Climate Well-Being Solutions
Focused on enhancing the climate well-being of communities, this pillar integrates design and functional elements into buildings that support both mental and social health, fostering environments that promote community interaction, comfort, and resilience.
03
Innovative Technological Systems
By integrating innovative tech systems, we aim to revolutionise production process, construction site management and property management incorporating advanced technologies such as AI and IoT to streamline operations and optimize the use of resources across all project phases.
